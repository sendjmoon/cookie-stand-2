OUR MISSION


Living in the Pacific Northwest can be tough with the limited amount of exposure we get to the sun. There are people reportedly suffering from seasonal depression because of the gloomy, gray skies. But fear no more! Smokin' Salmon Cookies are made with 100% real ingredients 80% of the time, all the time.

Our mission is to provide you with the missing nutrients and vitamins you need to carry on through our not-so-tough winters. Salmon provides vitamin B12, vitamin D, omega-3 fatty acids, and more! Join us in eating our way to a happier Northwest!
